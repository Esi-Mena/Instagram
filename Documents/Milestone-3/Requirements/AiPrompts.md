# AiPrompts Milestone-3
I use chat gpt to identify core features of the app wich i used for my product backlog.

# High Priority User Stories:
1. As a user, I want to register an account so that I have access to the app.

2. As a user, I want to log in and out of my account to control my access.

3. As a user, I want to upload photos to share with others.

4. As a user, I want to view posts from other users so that I can discover content.

5. As a user, I want to like posts to show my appreciation for them.

6. As a user, I want to follow other users to build my network and see their updates.

7. As a user, I want to leave comments on posts to interact with the community.

8. As a user, I want to receive notifications about likes, comments, and follows to stay engaged.

# Medium Priority User Stories:

1. As a user, I want to caption my photos to provide context and information.

2. As a user, I want to change my profile name to personalize my account.

3. As a user, I want to recover my password in case I forget it.

# Low Priority User Stories:

1. As a user, I want to find new accounts to follow and discover more content.

2. As a user, I want to search for other users to connect with specific accounts.

3. As a user, I want to block other users to manage my interactions.

4. As a user, I want to have a profile picture to personalize my profile.

5. As a user, I want to have a bio to provide information about myself.

6. As a user, I want to delete my account in case I decide to leave the platform.

7. As a user, I want a dark mode for a more comfortable viewing experience.
